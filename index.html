Send Gemstone Info to Discord
Paste your Discord webhook, select a gemstone and quality, then click to send

How to Send a Discord Webhook

  1. Go to your Discord server and create a webhook in any channel’s settings (Integrations → Webhooks).
  2. Copy the Webhook URL Discord gives you.
  3. Paste it into the Discord Webhook URL field above.
  4. Select your gemstone and quality, then click Send Discord Embed.
  5. Your Discord channel will receive a stylish embed with live gemstone info!
